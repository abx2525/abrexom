Common questions

Quick answers

Is your saffron really from Afghanistan? +

Yes. Every batch of Abrexom saffron is sourced directly from farming families in the Herat Valley, western Afghanistan. Each jar carries a batch number that traces it to a specific harvest. We do not blend saffron from multiple origins.

Where can I buy Abrexom saffron? +

Our saffron is currently available on Amazon and Etsy. We will be launching our own shop at abrexom.com soon. Sign up on the homepage to be notified when it goes live.

Do you do wholesale? +

We are open to wholesale enquiries from restaurants, specialty food retailers, and culinary institutions. Please use the contact form above and select "Wholesale enquiry" as the subject, and we will come back to you with details.

How should I store saffron? +

Store saffron in a cool, dark place away from moisture and direct light. Our airtight glass jar protects it well. Properly stored, saffron retains its potency for two to three years, though the flavour is most vibrant in the first year after harvest.
